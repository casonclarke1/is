The Violinist

Purest Form of Music

I have played the Violin since childhood. I can honestly say that it has shaped my life in unimaginable ways.

Thanks to that oh so noble instrument from the heavens I am better at math, appreciate the finer sounds in life, and have an ear for the inspiration that flows through music

I am also struck with awe at the men and women who have mastered the string instrument over the generations. Great people that could harness divinity in a single room. They were the true bringers of change. They were the champions of the hearts of men. We can but mourne the piece of us that has been lost to progress
